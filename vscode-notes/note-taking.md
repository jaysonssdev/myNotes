## Publish your Notes to Github

- Click the 'Source Control' button or 'Ctrl+Shift+G'
- Click 'Initialize Repository' button
- Type description. Example: '1st Commit'
- Click the 'Commit' button
- Click 'Public Branch'
- To not repeat these process over and over, use the 'GitDoc' extension.
    - Install GitDoc extension.
    - 'Ctrl+Shift+P' to see if it's enabled.
    - This also automatically saves your work every 30 seconds, change its settings if necessary.
